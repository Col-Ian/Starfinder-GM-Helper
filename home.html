Starfinder GM Helper

*Features do not work yet. This is just a guide to show all features that will work when completed. Further explanation of each individual page will be given upon completing their features.*

To help GMs running a Starfinder RPG session on the fly.

The Encounters page will generate a full encounter based on existing or randomly generated Starfinder NPCs, Creatures, or Starships.

The NPCs page will generate a random NPC/Creature based on various values.

The Loot page will generate loot for rewards from various source books.

The Starships page will generate a random Starship based on various values.
